The Lunch & Learn Session

One Lunch Hour.
Real Health Education That Sticks.

Dr. Marquez delivers a bilingual, culturally relevant health education session tailored specifically for Latino employees. Here's what your team will learn:

1

How to Live a Healthier Life in the U.S.

The transition from Latin America to the United States brings major changes in diet, lifestyle, and access to care. Dr. Marquez breaks it down in plain Spanish: what to eat, what to watch for, and how to build habits that work in an American environment.

2

Diseases and Health Risks From Back Home

Many conditions common in Latin America — parasites, tropical illnesses, viruses, and metabolic conditions tied to regional diets — are rarely screened for in standard U.S. checkups. Employees will learn what to watch for and when to get checked.

3

Food, Nutrition, and Real Preventive Care

Culturally relevant nutrition guidance that respects how Latino families eat and live, with practical advice employees can start using that same day — no generic handouts, no one-size-fits-all advice.

Common Questions

Everything HR Directors Want to Know

Is there any cost to my company to host a Lunch & Learn? +
No. The Lunch & Learn program is provided at no charge to your business. You provide the space and your employees' time during lunch. We bring the doctor, the presentation, and the complimentary lab test vouchers for every attendee.
What is included in the free Perfil 20 lab panel? +
Every employee who attends receives a voucher for three key lab tests: Hematología Completa (Complete Blood Count), Perfil Metabólico (Metabolic Panel), and Perfil de Lípidos (Lipid Panel). Together, these tests are valued at over $150 per person.
Do my employees need health insurance to use the lab coupon? +
No. Centro Médico Familiar serves patients with or without insurance. Employees who redeem their lab voucher will be taken care of regardless of their coverage status. Con o sin seguro, atendemos.
What language is the session presented in? +
The presentation is delivered in Spanish by Dr. Orlin Marquez and his team, who are native Spanish speakers from Latin America. Portions can also be provided in English if needed. There are no interpreters — just direct, clear communication.
How long does the Lunch & Learn session last? +
The typical session is designed to fit within a standard 45 to 60 minute lunch break. We are flexible and can adjust to fit your team's schedule and available space.
How many employees need to attend to qualify? +
We work with companies of all sizes — from 10 Spanish-speaking employees to 100+. There is no minimum headcount required to book. We tailor the session to your group size.
Can you serve employees who have never seen a doctor in the U.S. before? +
Absolutely. That is exactly who this program was designed for. Many Latino employees avoid U.S. healthcare because of language barriers, insurance confusion, or distrust of the system. Dr. Marquez and his team understand that experience and serve this community with patience and respect.
Can employees become patients at the clinic after the session? +
Yes. Employees who attend and want to continue their care are welcome to schedule appointments at Centro Médico Familiar in Duluth, Georgia. The clinic accepts most major insurance plans and also offers accessible self-pay options for those without coverage.
What happens after I fill out the form? +
A coordinator from Centro Médico Familiar will contact you within 48 business hours to answer your questions, confirm details about your team size and location, and get your Lunch & Learn officially scheduled. There is no obligation after filling out the form.
